## Feedback

If you find a bug or have suggestions, you are welcome to contact the author and help improve UzonCalc.

For bug reports, please use [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/uyoufu/UzonCalc/issues) first so they can be tracked clearly.

For urgent issues, please contact by email.

QQ and QQ group messages are checked irregularly, so replies may not be immediate.
